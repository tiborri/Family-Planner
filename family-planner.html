Beach day — Sandbanks Beach, Poole
One of the best beaches in Britain — fine white sand, shallow water, Blue Flag every year since 1987, lifeguards on duty, café and beach shop on site, toilets, and a large 532-space pay-and-display car park right next to the beach. Drive is ~1h 10m via the A31/A348.

🔥 Heatwave day — forecast ~28–30°C: the early start is critical. Sun is brutal by 11am, the sand scorching underfoot, and the car park will be full by mid-morning. 🚗 Leave home 7:30–8:00am — on the sand by ~9:00 while it's still pleasant. Plan to be in the shade or back in the car by ~12:30 before the worst heat and the queue out.

🌊 Tide note: Sandbanks has a gently shelving beach — at low tide there's a huge expanse of sand, perfect for digging and paddling. The Solent sea will be warmer than the Channel (~15–16°C). 📍 Open in Maps

🅿️ Parking: pay-and-display, no pre-booking needed — 532 spaces at Banks Road BH13 7QQ, open 24hrs. Pay via cash, JustPark app or RingGo. Arrive before 9am to guarantee a space; in a heatwave it fills by late morning. No need to book ahead — just arrive early.

☀️ Heat safety with the boys: reapply factor 50 every 90 min and after every dip, UV swim tops stay on, parasol or shelter for naps, water every 15–20 min (not just when they ask). Watch for red cheeks, headaches, unusual quiet — first signs of heat stress.

🔄 Cook-once logic
  • Monday: cook double rice → Thursday fried rice is half done
  • Both avocados bought Monday are ready Tue + Sat
  • Sunday tray bake: roast a few extra potato chunks for Monday lunchboxes
🥒 Sunomono in 3 minutes
  • Slice cucumber thin or ribbon with a peeler
  • Mix: 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp sugar, pinch of salt
  • Toss, rest 5 min, add sesame seeds on top
  • Makes ahead — keeps in fridge for 2 days
🧒 Kid-friendly tactics
  • Let them pop their own edamame pods — they love it
  • A little soy sauce or ketchup on the side goes a long way
  • If a meal is refused: cucumber and rice. They won't go hungry
  • Friday fish fingers is a mental break for everyone — own it
🥗 Nutrition check
  • Every dinner has protein + carb + vegetable
  • Salmon Tuesday covers omega-3s for the week
  • Edamame gives plant protein and iron
  • Broccoli and carrots rotate through for vitamins A and C
  • Dessert: fruit Mon/Wed, yogurt Tue/Thu, lollies Fri/Sat/Sun
🍝 Pasta & sauce tips
  • Salt the pasta water generously — it matters
  • Blitz veg smooth into the bolognese; kids won't notice
  • Keep a ladle of pasta water to loosen the sauce
  • Smaller shapes (penne, fusilli) are easier for the 3yo than spaghetti
🛒 Shopping strategy
  • Buy both avocados on Monday — they ripen over the week
  • One shop covers the week: meat & fish Mon, top up veg Thu if needed
  • Pre-cut veg on Sunday and keep in the fridge for speed
  • Salmon: ask the fishmonger to skin if you don't fancy doing it
