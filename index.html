🔄 Cook-once logic
  • Monday: cook double rice → Thursday fried rice is already half done
  • Sunday tray bake: roast extra potato chunks → Monday lunchboxes
  • Buy both avocados on Monday — they ripen over the week, ready by Wednesday
  • Week 1 starts with almost zero shopping — the fridge and freezer do the work
🥒 Sunomono in 3 minutes
  • Slice cucumber thin or ribbon with a peeler
  • Mix: 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp sugar, pinch of salt
  • Toss, rest 5 min, add sesame seeds on top
  • Makes ahead — keeps in fridge for 2 days
🧒 Kid-friendly tactics
  • Let them pop their own edamame pods — they love it
  • A little soy sauce or ketchup on the side goes a long way for the boys — adults can use chilli or sriracha
  • If a meal is refused: cucumber and rice. They won't go hungry
  • Friday fish fingers is a deliberate strategy, not a failure — own it
  • Pizza Saturday: lay out toppings and let them build their own
🥗 Nutrition check
  • Every dinner aims for protein + carb + at least 2 veg (use frozen mixed veg to make this effortless)
  • Salmon appears twice a week from W2 onwards — strong omega‑3 coverage
  • Wednesday bolognese is upgraded with 50/50 beef + lentils to boost fibre and reduce saturated fat
  • Sunday tray bake rotates an extra veg (pepper / courgette / red onion) to increase variety across the week
  • Treat rhythm: keep ice lollies to ~2× per week; use fruit or yogurt + berries on other nights
🍝 Pasta tips
  • Always cook fresh — pasta goes mushy when frozen or reheated, so it's never batched
  • Salt the pasta water generously — it matters more than any other step
  • Blitz the veg smooth into the bolognese; the boys won't notice a thing
  • Keep a ladle of pasta water to loosen the sauce at the end
  • Fusilli or penne is easier for the 3yo than spaghetti
🛒 Shopping strategy
  • Week 1 (27 May): 4 items max — nearly everything is already at home
  • One main shop covers Mon–Sun with minimal mid-week top-ups
  • Chicken appears Mon, Thu and Sun — buy 700g once, covers all three
  • Frozen peas and potato chunks: buy once, covers all three weeks
  • Add 1 bag frozen mixed veg + 1 pack lentils — they upgrade multiple meals with almost no extra effort
  • Pizza nights: plan 2 veg toppings (sweetcorn + peppers/mushrooms) so the ritual stays balanced
